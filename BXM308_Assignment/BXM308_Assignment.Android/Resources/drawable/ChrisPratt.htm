The Way Chris Pratt Uses a Beautyblender Is So Funny It Hurts

By Christopher Rosa

December 13, 2016

      Let's get one thing straight: Chris Pratt is a literal angel sent from above to make us laugh, sigh, and smile like it's Taco Tuesday every day. His comedic brilliance knows no bounds: From expertly trolling Jennifer Lawrence to performing magic on talk shows, Pratt knows how to keep us entertained and, frankly, thirsty.

      And now he's at it again. The Guardians of the Galaxy hUnk (I find the word "hunk" hilarious and like to use it in silly ways) uploaded a video of himself getting his makeup done while on a press tour for his new film, Passengers. However, Pratt isn't cool with just sitting back and letting a makeup artist take the reins. Nope. This versatile actor likes to take matters into his own hands by incessantly bopping his head as his hairstylist, Bridget Brager, holds a makeup sponge in place—essentially doing the glam himself. If that's not a revolutionary way to use a sponge, then what the hell is?

      "When I got delirious on these press tours I like to do my own makeup by having @bridgetbragerhair hold the makeup sponge and I dance my face pretty," Pratt wrote on Instagram. "Thank you to @theweeknd for writing this song which if I tweak the lyrics a little bit allows me to pretend it's about #starlord #passengersmovie." See the adorable video for yourself, below. As if we all needed another reason to be hopelessly in love with Chris Pratt.

      This also isn't Pratt's first time at the beauty rodeo. In fact, his glam game is better than all of ours. He is an expert at braiding wife Anna Faris' hair; he also knows his way around a sheet mask. A man after our own hearts, to be honest.

      He's the beauty icon we didn't know we needed—and certainly don't deserve. Swoon.
